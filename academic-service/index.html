Academic Service

Here is a list of activities that I do as a part time basis. With these activities, I realized that academics is the proper place for one to live and enjoy helping others and sharing valuable knowledge.

Remote Attendance Chair VCBM 2021
Served as a Remote Attendance Chair at the 11th EuroGraphics Workshop on Visual Computing for Biology and Medicine (VCBM) and coordinated the conduct of workshop held during September 22-24, 2021 in Paris, France.
https://conferences.eg.org/vcbm2021/chairs-committees/
Journal Reviewer
Serving as a Journal Reviewer in International Journals and Conferences.

IEEE-CAS Outreach Workshop on Machine Intelligence – 2012
Served as a Workshop Committee member and coordinated the conduct of workshop at IIITM-K on 18-20 May 2012. The workshop attracted several school students and teachers in Kerala.
Free Lectures: 2nd IEEE-CAS Outreach Workshop on Machine Intelligence – 2014
Handled lecture sessions in Robotics and Image Processing at different schools in Kerala, India.
Computer Vision Application Development with OpenCV
Handled a three day lecture on topics: Image Processing, Augmented Reality, Fundamentals of OpenCV and Computer Vision in Android using OpenCV. The workshop was organized by International Centre for Free and Open Source Software (ICFOSS), India on 17-19 July 2014 at Malabar Hall, Technopark. The workshop received a wide range of audience from academia and industry from Technopark, Trivandrum.
https://icfoss.in/event-details/23
Robotics Primer – One day workshop on Robotics
Handled lecture sessions in an one-day workshop on Robotics at IIITM-K, India on 10th May 2014. Topics handled include Basic Robotics, Embedded C and Arduino.
Guest Lecturer
Worked as a Guest Lecturer at Centre for Career Development Services, Trivandrum, India. Topics Handled: C Programming and UNIX.
